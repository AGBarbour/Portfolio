Max Temperature

"The data reflects what one would expect. The warmest temperatures were observed nearer to the equator, and the colder temperatures were observed further from the equator. The apex of the curve is shifted 20-30 degrees north of the equator with peak temperatures around 30 degrees which is due in part to the date of observation. The data was collected during the summer season of Northern Hemisphere."
